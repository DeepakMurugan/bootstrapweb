MONOFIX Resin binder for synthetic yarn
MONOSOFT Sizing softener to increase the softness, flexibility and smoothness of yarn

MONOBOND SR 500

MONOBOND SR 500 is a binder with an exceptionally high adhesive power. This product is suitable for the latest high speed air jet and water jet looms

This product is an acrylic co polymer binder with very low viscosity .This product is suitable for all counts of yarn made out of Cotton, Viscose ,Polyester Cotton blends and Viscose Cotton blends.


Key Features & Benefits

PVA Free No presence of PVA & eco friendly
Versatile Suitalbe for all types of fibre, yarn sizing & weaving machine
Compatible Compatible with PVA, resin binders, acrylic binders, CMC, softeners, wetting agents & wax
Properties Low viscosity, excellent abrasion resistance & pliability

TECHNICAL DATA

Apperarance Solution
Viscosity 7500 CPS @ 25
Solid Content 25%
Misciblity Easily miscible in water.

Application Guideline

Monobond SR 500 is easily dispersilble and can be used during cooking, at the storage tank or in the sow box . It is recommended to add upto 4 kgs per cook .


Monofix

Monofix is a binder designed to bind any form of protruding fibres in synthetic yarn to its core. This product enhances the cohesive strength of the synthetic yarn better.

This product is suitable for all types of yarn.


Key Features & Benefits

PVA Free No Presence of PVA
Versatile Suitable for all types of yarn and fibres
Compatible Compatible with all types for starch, binders and softeners.
Properties Medium viscosity, excellent cohesive strength.

TECHNICAL DATA

Solid Content 50%
Specific Gravity 1.25
pH 4.5 to 5

Application Guideline

Monofix is easily dispersible during cooking and can be added during cooking and post cooking too. Maximum recommended quantity per cook is up to 4 Kgs.


MONOSOFT

Monosoft is an extremely effective natural softener added in the size paste to make the yarn pliable and brittle free when the yarn is woven at very high speeds.

Monosoft also acts as a hygroscopic agent and prevents the yarn from becoming brittle due to over drying. It also acts as a defoamer to prevent foaming in size mix which occurs due to change in water pH.


Key Features & Benefits

Properties Excellent abrasion resistance property.
Uniqueness Acts as an emulsifier, lubricant and anti bacterial agent
Efficient Makes the yarn highly pliable to withstand bending & flexing during weaving.
Multi Functional Can be used as softener, lubricant, emulsifier, hygroscopic agent and defoamer .

TECHNICAL DATA

Apperarance Paste
Colour Off White
pH 6-7
Specific Gravity 1.04

Application Guideline

Monosoft is easily dispersible and it is recommended to add Monosoft at the storage tank after the size paste is prepared.


It is recommended to add up to 4 Kgs per cook of size paste


MONOSTAT SR 250

Monostat SR 250 is a synthetic antistatic agent designed to provide protection to the synthetic blended yarn during weaving.

Monostat SR 250 is resistant to oxidation with low viscosity.


Key Features & Benefits

Versatality Can be applied through pad trough, spraying & conventional methods
Stability Resistant to oxidation, low volatility & excellent heat stability
Uniqueness Does not require curing, effective on polyamides, polyesters, acrylics, olefins and natural fibres

TECHNICAL DATA

Apperarance Paste
Colour Off White
pH 6-7
Specific Gravity 1.04

Application Guideline

It is recommended to use Monostat SR 250 at the storage tank only and not during the process of cooking.


It is recommended to use Monostat SR 250 during sizing of synthetic or synthetic blended yarn.


Upto 4 Kgs per cook can be used for better results.


MONOCON

MONOCON is a wetting agent designed to reduce the critical surface tension on the yarn during cooking thereby making a thorough and rapid displacement of air from the surface of the solid and thereby making penetration of various chemicals more uniform. Monocon is a non ionic wetting agent which wets the fibre more rapidly by diffusing through its surface.


Key Features & Benefits

  • Has high wetting power
  • No adverse effect on other auxiliaries
  • Stable at high temperatures
  • No turbidity, deposits on fibre or machine parts
  • Excellent emulsifier
  • Free from heavy metals

TECHNICAL DATA

Apperarance Clear Liquid
pH 6.5-7.0
Ionic Status Non Ionic
Miscibility Fully miscible in water

Application Guideline

Monocon is easily dispersible in water


It is recommended to add Monocon initially to water before adding any material in the process of cooking .


Upto 200gm per cook can be used for better results .
