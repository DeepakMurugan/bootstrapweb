MONARCH BL

Monarch BL is a specially formulated modified starch for fabrics made in the Sulzer and power looms.This product has excellent adhesion to Cotton, Polyester, Viscose fibres and its blends.

Monarch BL gives better bonding, excellent lubricity, flexibility and abrasion resistance making it an ideal choice in yarn sizing.

Monarch BL does not lead to any objectionable build up on equipment or substrate


Key Features & Benefits

PVA Free No presence of PVA & eco friendly
Versatile Suitalbe for all types of fibre, yarn sizing & weaving machine
Compatible Compatible with PVA, resin binders, acrylic binders, CMC, softeners, wetting agents & wax
Properties Low viscosity, excellent abrasion resistance & pliability

Technical Data

pH 7.3
Moisture 11%
Ash Content 0.1-0.2%

Application Guideline

Monarch BL is an ideal modified starch for reducing cost & enhancing the performance in Sulzer & power looms


For hairiness control in Cotton sorts it is better to add binder upto 4% of the weight of starch


For P/c, Viscose and blends, it is recommended to add antistatic upto 4% on the weight of starch for better binding and hairiness control on yarn.


Standard open cooker/pressure cooker normally available can be used.


MONARCH

MONARCH is a specially formulated single shot textile sizing product designed for sizing counts upto 40’s Ne. This product is best suited for sizing Cotton, Polyester Cotton blends and Viscose Cotton blends.

Monarch gives excellent coating and also helps in binding the protruding fibres on the surface of the yarn with optimum size penetration.


Key Features & Benefits

Properties Optimum viscosity that provides a balance between coating and penetration
Suppleness It makes the yarn adapt with ease to the heavy beating and abrasion during weaving
Green Labelled Just the right amount of solution for all environmental & waste water requirements

Technical Data

pH 7-7.5
Moisture 11%
Ash Content 0.1

Application Guideline

Monarch is ideally suited for counts below 40’s Ne for any type of sizing, weaving machine and any fabric sow box cover factor.


It is best recommended for sizing carded, combed and compact yarn.


For P/c, Viscose, and blends, it is recommended to add antistatic upto 4% on the weight of starch for better binding and hairiness control on yarn.


Standard open cooker/pressure cooker normally available can be used

MONARCH SPR

MONARCH SPR is a unique product designed for sizing 50’s Ne high density fabrics and 60’s Ne yarn.

The specialty of this product is the high binding property and high yarn abrasion resistance resulting in high loom efficiency at very high speeds.

All these are obtained without compromising the suppleness of the yarn or its elongation


Key Features & Benefits

Pliability High loom speeds, low breakage rate
Low Add On Low cost, minimum invisible losses & high utilization.

Technical Data

pH 6.5
Moisture 12.5%
Ash Content 0.5%-0.75%

Application Guideline

Monarch SPR is ideally suited for counts 50’s Ne (High density fabrics) & 60’s Ne for any type of sizing, weaving machine and any fabric sow box cover factor.


It is best recommended for sizing combed and compact yarn


For P/c, Viscose, and blends, it is recommended to add antistatic upto 4% on the weight of starch for better binding and hairiness control on yarn.

MONARCH COMBO

Monarch Combo is a premium product designed with a new sizing module that helps in better size application on very fine yarns.

The uniqueness of this product is the uniform size application and lower loss on elongation on super fine yarn especially on latest technology sizing machines where the yarn is not immersed in size liquor.


Key Features & Benefits

Low Viscosity Great size penetration, optimum coating.
High Strength High efficiency, abrasion resistance and high productivity.
Smoothness Superior fabric feel, high yarn pliability and very low elongation loss.

Technical Data

pH 6.5
Moisture 15%
Ash Content 0.96%

Application Guideline

Monarch Combo is ideally suited for counts 80’s Ne to 120’s


It is best recommended for sizing compact yarn in both regular and Pro size sizing machines with Indian Cotton, Supima Cotton and Egyptian Cotton fibres.


For P/c, Viscose, and blends, it is recommended to add antistatic upto 4% on the weight of starch for better binding and hairiness control on yarn.

Standard open cooker/pressure cooker normally available can be used
